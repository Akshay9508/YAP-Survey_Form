Career Guidance Seminar

YAP (Youth Awareness Program), an initiative by the youth of Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organization, is designed around the five human values of Truth, Righteousness, Peace, Love, and Non-violence. YAP has been in existence for over 20 years in more than 40 colleges in the city of Mumbai, in which thousands of students participated in various value-based activities. The idea of YAP's new initiative, Career Guidance Seminar, is to help young adults take informed decisions on their career paths.
